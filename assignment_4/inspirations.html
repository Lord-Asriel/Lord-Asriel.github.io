Valve Software

https://thumbor.forbes.com/thumbor/960x0/https%3A%2F%2Fblogs-images.forbes.com%2Ferikkain%2Ffiles%2F2017%2F10%2Forange-box.jpg

Valve is a software company based in Bellevue Washingon. This company is responsible for many impressive feats of computer software including Steam, the largest PC gaming platform.

Besides revolutionising the entire video game industry with this platform, Valve is responsible for some of the most renown video games of all time including Portal 2, Half-Life 2: Episode 1, Half-Life 2: Episode 2, Half-Life 2 and Portal.

These games are notable for their engines, which when released where better than anything available in the market.

This company inspires me as they have demonstrated to me how powerful computer science can be as a means of entertainment and storytelling

Undertale

https://cdn.cgmagonline.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/08/undertale-reveals-nintendo-switch-release-date-8.jpg

Undertale is a video game released in 2015 and created by Toby Fox.

Undertale has achieved success for its immersive storytelling, creative combat and morality system which has resulted in a very dedicated, almost obsessive fanbase.

This game inspired me to get into computer science. It showed me the true storytelling potential of the medium, and demonstrated that if I put the effort in I could make my own game, as Toby Fox himself used a rudementary engine called GameMaker.

It demonstartes that even with the most limited of tools, it is possible to do something technologically amazing.

Tool Assisted Speedrun

https://www.rockpapershotgun.com/images/16/jan/setup.jpg/RPSS/resize/760x-1/format/jpg/quality/90

A video game speedrun is when a player attempts to get to the end, or an agreed point in a video game, as fast as possible. There are large and organised speedrunning communities online.

Speedruns range from playing the game normally, to exploited errors within the game's code, or glitches.

A Tool Assisted Speedrun is when there is no longer a human player attempting the speedrun, but instead a tool which has a desired input mapped to each of the game's frames. This allows for extreme accuracy and much faster speeds than a human could achieve.

A TAS is extremely impressive to watch, as exploits that take a lot of skill to master are performed flawlessly by a machine. It truly shows the power of somebody willing to look deep into the code.

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure

https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/proxy/da7P7rt7P9IQKmo0zPDzyzOV9DZhrNf4QxADDfIbZQ1sm9M4NVukX6W6G_2mX7LE6NWyYTfrKLfjHxDlLnZetQ3rSkljt8aJkNSnb7at

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure is a popular manga and anime series created by Hirohiko Araki and animated by David Productions.

This story tells the generational tale of the Joestar bloodline and their fight against evil.

The manga currently has eight parts, with each being following a different generation. The first five parts have been animated.

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure is an amazing piece of animation and one of the culminations of computer science as an art form. It represents the frontier of visual technology, and its continuation has been stunted by the currently available animation software, as its seventh part is currently too expensive tp create.
